Offering courses for FREE

Punjab Vocational Training Council (PVTC) is the largest vocational training provider in Pakistan through its 208 Vocational Training Institutes (VTIs) established across Punjab and VTI Fateh Jang is one of these institutes. PVTC was set up by Government of the Punjab in 1998 and we have graduated more than 800,000 trainees in 69 different demand driven trades. We solemnly believe in the practical part of vocational training, thus 2 months On Job Training (OJT) is mandatory for each trainee.
